About CareerBridge Industrial Attachment Portal

Connecting students with organizations for meaningful industrial attachment opportunities.

Who we are

CareerBridge is a web-based industrial attachment portal designed to connect students, universities, and employers through meaningful real-world career opportunities. The platform simplifies the process of finding and managing industrial attachments by allowing students to browse available placements, apply for opportunities, and track their application status in one centralized system. Employers can easily advertise internship positions and review student applications, while universities are able to support and monitor the placement process. CareerBridge bridges the gap between academic learning and professional practice, helping students gain valuable industry experience before entering the workforce.

Students can browse opportunities, apply for placements, and track their application status through a simple dashboard.

Employers can review applications, accept or reject students, and manage available attachment positions easily.

Students

Browse available industrial attachment opportunities and apply directly through the portal.

Employers

Post internship opportunities and select the best candidates for your organization.

Universities

Monitor student placement progress and ensure successful industrial training experiences.

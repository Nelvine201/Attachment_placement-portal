About CareerBridge

Empowering students and organizations through seamless industrial attachment placement.

Who We Are

CareerBridge is a modern web-based industrial attachment portal designed to connect students, universities, and employers in one integrated platform. Our goal is to simplify and digitize the attachment process, making it efficient, transparent, and accessible to all stakeholders.

Through CareerBridge, students can discover relevant opportunities, submit applications, and track their progress in real time. Employers are empowered to post opportunities, review applications, and select the most suitable candidates, while institutions can monitor and support student placement.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and professional experience by providing a reliable and efficient industrial attachment platform.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital platform for industrial attachment and internship placement across universities and organizations.

Who We Serve

Students

Access verified attachment opportunities, apply easily, and track applications through a centralized dashboard.

Employers

Publish opportunities, review applicants, and efficiently manage recruitment for industrial attachments.

Institutions

Monitor student placements, ensure compliance, and support successful training outcomes.

Whether you're a student looking for experience or an employer seeking talent, CareerBridge makes the connection simple and effective.
